What We Do

We think finding information about how to transfer your nursing license, renew, and seek continuing education requirements should be easy. Unfortunately, many state websites don't organize their information in a way that's easy to locate. We have presented the information you are looking for in a uniform way and we provide links to the primary sources of information so you can verify what we have posted on this site. We also provided a way to get CE credit on the bottom of each page on the site.
